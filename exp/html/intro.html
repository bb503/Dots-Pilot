Dot Estimate Task



We want to see how people provide numerical estimates. In this study, you will be presented with a number of dots in an array. The task is simple: without counting, your job is to guess the number of dots in the array. You will be given feedback on your performance.


This is part of a study being run at the University of Adelaide. By clicking "Next" below you consent to take part in this study.
